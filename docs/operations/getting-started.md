# Getting Started

This guide covers the basics of accessing MycoERP, signing in, and finding your way around the application.

## Accessing the Application

Open MycoERP in a web browser. The application works best in modern browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. It is responsive and works on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile phones.

## Signing In

When you open the application, you will see the login screen.

1. Enter your email address in the "Email" field.
2. Enter your password in the "Password" field.
3. Click "Sign In".

If your account is active and your credentials are correct, you will be taken to the Dashboard.

## Creating a New Account

If you do not yet have an account, click "Sign Up" on the login screen.

1. Enter your full name.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Choose a password (minimum 6 characters).
4. Select your role from the dropdown. If you are unsure, select "Production Worker" -- your administrator can change this later.
5. Select your department (Lab, Spawn, Substrate, Incubation, Fruiting, Harvest, Packaging, or Management).
6. Click "Create Account".

After signing up, you will be logged in automatically.

## The Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home screen. It shows at a glance:

- **Active Batches** -- Total number of batches currently in production.
- **Tasks Due Today** -- Work items that need attention today.
- **Overdue Tasks** -- Tasks that have passed their due date without completion.
- **Critical Tasks** -- High-urgency items that need immediate attention.
- **Pipeline Overview** -- A visual flow of how many batches are at each production stage (Culture, LC, Spawn, Substrate, Fruiting).
- **Environmental Alerts** -- Any active alerts from IoT sensors that require acknowledgement.
- **Today's Tasks** -- A list of tasks assigned to you or your role group that are due today.
- **Upcoming Harvests** -- Fruiting batches that are approaching their expected ready date.
- **QR Activity** -- Number of QR scans performed today.

## Navigating the Sidebar

The left sidebar provides access to all areas of the system. It is organized into collapsible groups:

**Main**
- Dashboard -- The overview screen described above.

**Production**
- Batches -- View and manage all production batches.
- Tasks -- View and manage your work assignments.
- Contamination -- Report and track contamination incidents.
- Harvests -- Record and review harvest yields.
- Environmental -- Log and view environmental readings.

**Resources**
- Inventory -- Track supplies and consumables.
- Rooms -- View physical locations and their conditions.
- Species/Strains -- Browse the mushroom catalog.
- SOP Templates -- View the standard operating procedure templates.
- QR Codes -- Generate and manage QR labels.

**Reporting**
- Reports -- View production analytics and trends.

**Admin** (visible only to Admin and Manager roles)
- Users -- Manage user accounts.
- Permissions -- Configure role-based access.
- Settings -- System-wide configuration.
- IoT Devices -- Manage sensor hardware.

The sidebar collapses on mobile devices. Tap the menu icon in the top-left to open it.

## Switching Languages

MycoERP is available in English and Bengali. To switch languages:

1. Look for the language indicator in the top header bar.
2. Click it to toggle between English and Bengali.
3. The entire interface will update immediately. Your language preference is saved and will persist when you return.

## Notifications

The bell icon in the top header shows your unread notification count. Click it to see:

- Task assignments and due date reminders.
- Environmental alerts that need acknowledgement.
- Task approvals or rejections.
- Contamination reports filed on your batches.

Click a notification to navigate directly to the relevant item.

## Understanding Your Role

What you can see and do in the application depends on your assigned role:

- If you cannot see a menu item or a button, it means your role does not have permission for that action.
- If you see a section but cannot create or edit within it, your role has view-only access to that area.
- If you believe you need additional access, contact your farm administrator.

Your current role and name are displayed in the sidebar footer.

## Quick Actions

From the Dashboard, you can:

- Click "New Batch" to start a new production batch (if your role allows).
- Click any task in the "Today's Tasks" list to view and complete it.
- Click an environmental alert to acknowledge and investigate it.
- Click any pipeline stage to see batches at that stage.
- Use the QR scanner shortcut to quickly scan a QR label.
